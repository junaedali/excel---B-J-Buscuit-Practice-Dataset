# B&J Biscuit Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents a complete **B&J Biscuit Sales Analysis Dashboard** created using Microsoft Excel. The dashboard analyzes biscuit sales performance based on revenue, profit, COGS, quantity purchased, customer profile, location, payment method, product type, and time-based performance changes.

The project includes three main sections:

1. Main Sales Dashboard  
2. Change Analysis Dashboard  
3. Insights Report  

The main objective of this project is to convert sales data into meaningful business insights and help management understand profitable products, customers, locations, and sales patterns.

## Dashboard 1 Insights

### Revenue by Customer

The dashboard shows customer-wise revenue contribution. Robert generated the highest revenue share, making him the most valuable customer in this view.

### Revenue by Age Group

Revenue was analyzed across different age groups:

- 15–29
- 30–44
- 45–59
- 60–74
- 75–89

The dashboard shows that older age groups contributed strongly to revenue, especially the 60–74 and 75–89 groups.

### Revenue by Payment Method

Revenue was compared across different payment methods:

- Cash
- Credit Card
- Debit Card
- Mobile Payment

All payment methods contributed almost equally, which means the business does not depend heavily on only one payment method.

### Revenue by Location

The dashboard compares revenue across multiple locations such as San Jose, San Diego, San Antonio, Phoenix, Philadelphia, New York, Los Angeles, Houston, Dallas, and Chicago.

This section helps identify which cities are generating the highest revenue and which locations may need further improvement.

### Revenue by Gender

| Gender | Revenue Share |
|---|---:|
| Male | 85% |
| Female | 9% |
| Other | 6% |

Male customers contributed the largest portion of revenue, making them the dominant customer segment in this dashboard.

### Revenue by Price Type

| Price Type | Revenue Share |
|---|---:|
| Less Expensive | 89% |
| Expensive | 11% |

Most revenue comes from less expensive products. This indicates that affordable biscuit products are the main revenue driver.

## Dashboard 2: Change Analysis Dashboard

The second dashboard focuses on time-based performance changes. It includes:

- Quarter-over-quarter analysis
- Month-over-month analysis
- Week-over-week analysis

This section helps understand whether revenue is increasing, decreasing, or remaining stable over time.

---

## Change Analysis Insights

### Quarter-over-Quarter Analysis

Quarterly performance shows that the first two quarters performed strongly, while Q3 and Q4 declined significantly.

| Quarter | Contribution |
|---|---:|
| Q1 | 36% |
| Q2 | 35% |
| Q3 | 19% |
| Q4 | 10% |

This indicates that the business performed well in the first half of the year but experienced a decline in the second half.

### Month-over-Month Analysis

The monthly analysis shows that revenue was relatively stable from January to July. However, revenue declined sharply from August to December.

This indicates that the business may need stronger marketing campaigns, seasonal offers, or customer retention strategies during the later months of the year.

### Week-over-Week Analysis

The week-over-week chart shows stable weekly performance across five weeks. Each week shows similar contribution, indicating that short-term weekly revenue movement remained consistent.

## Key Insights from the Report

1. **Shortbread is the most profitable product.**  
   This product generated the highest profit among all biscuit products.

2. **San Antonio is the top revenue city.**  
   San Antonio generated the highest revenue among all locations.

3. **The best-performing age group is 60–74.**  
   This age group contributed the highest revenue.

4. **Male customers contribute the most revenue.**  
   Male customers are the dominant customer segment.

5. **Revenue mainly comes from less expensive products.**  
   Affordable products generate the majority of revenue.

6. **Sales dropped significantly in Q3 and Q4.**  
   The business performed strongly in Q1 and Q2 but declined in the second half of the year.

7. **Debit card brings slightly higher revenue.**  
   Among payment methods, debit card generated slightly higher revenue compared to others.

## Business Recommendations

Based on the dashboard findings, the following recommendations can be made:

1. Promote **Shortbread** more aggressively because it is the most profitable product.
2. Focus on high-performing locations such as **San Antonio** and **Chicago**.
3. Design targeted campaigns for the **60–74** and older customer age groups.
4. Create customer retention strategies for high-value customers such as **Robert**.
5. Continue offering affordable products because less expensive products generate most of the revenue.
6. Investigate the reason behind the revenue decline in Q3 and Q4.
7. Use seasonal campaigns and discounts to improve sales during low-performing months.
8. Maintain multiple payment options because revenue is balanced across payment methods.

---

## Conclusion

The B&J Biscuit Sales Analysis Dashboard provides a complete overview of business performance across customers, products, locations, age groups, payment methods, and time periods.

The dashboard shows that Shortbread is the most profitable product, male customers generate the highest revenue, less expensive products drive most sales, and revenue performance declined significantly in the second half of the year.

This project demonstrates how Microsoft Excel can be used to create an interactive business intelligence dashboard and generate practical insights for sales and marketing decision-making.
